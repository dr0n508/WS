TriVita Clinic of Integrative Medicine

TriVita Clinic of Integrative Medicine, located at corporate headquarters in Scottsdale, AZ, specializes in personalized, teambased primary care that goes beyond prescriptions. Patients come from all over North America for the latest advancements in detection and treatment. TriVita Clinic offers conventional, complementary and alternative healthcare dedicated to the early detection, prevention and reversal of lifestyle-related diseases.

TriVita Clinic of Integrative Medicine specializes in:

  • Comprehensive Primary Care
  • Women’s Health
  • Pain Management
  • Brain and Heart Health
  • Allergies and Asthma
  • Hormone Therapy
  • Regenerative Medicine
  • Telemedicine

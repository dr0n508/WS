Giving Back

At the House of Giving, our purpose is to simply give back to those less fortunate. Since its inception in 2001, House of Giving has provided significant contributions and resources to communities in the United States and other international communities in need.

We are registered 501(C-3) and 100% of the donations we receive go directly to projects designated by our donors.

Our projects focus on basic pillars for social and economic development such as; nutrition, clean water, education and adolescent team sports.
